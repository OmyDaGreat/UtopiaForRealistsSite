The Power of Free Money

A critical look at Universal Basic Income not as a handout, but as the ultimate venture capital investment in human potential.

The Concept

Imagine a floor below which no one can fall. Universal Basic Income (UBI) provides a regular, unconditional cash payment to every citizen. It strips away the complex bureaucracy of conditional welfare.

FACT

It's an investment in stability, allowing individuals to take risks, educate themselves, and negotiate better wages without the fear of destitution.
