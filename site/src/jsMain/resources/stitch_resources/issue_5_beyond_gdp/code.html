BEYOND GDP

For decades, we’ve worshipped a single, deeply flawed metric. Gross Domestic Product tells us how fast the machine is running, but it is entirely blind to whether the machine is crushing us in its gears.

BROKEN!

The Illusion of Growth

GDP measures the total value of goods and services produced. It is a brilliant accounting tool from the 1930s. But treating it as the ultimate proxy for human well-being is a tragic error.

When a forest is clear-cut, GDP goes up. When a massive oil spill requires billion-dollar cleanup efforts, GDP goes up. When a populace becomes sicker and spends more on healthcare, GDP goes up. It counts the bad with the good, entirely ignoring the underlying health of society.
