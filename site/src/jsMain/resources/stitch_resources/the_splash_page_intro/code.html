The Origin Story local_library

We live in a time of unprecedented wealth, yet our imaginations for the future have seemingly stalled. The great dreams of the past—ending poverty, dramatically reducing the working week, opening borders—have been dismissed as naive fantasies.

But what if these "utopian" ideas are not only possible but completely realistic? This manifest challenges the status quo, arguing that progress shouldn't stop at mere survival. It's time to demand a life of meaning, leisure, and true liberty for all.
